Lynel Horns are Monster Parts that are dropped by all types of Lynels upon their defeats. They're too hard for most blades to scratch, but they can be combined with critters in a Cooking Pot to make Elixirs. They can also be used at the Kochi Dye Shop to make White Dye or exchanged for Mon at the Fang and Bone.
